Notable Activities:

HEALTHY:
- contribute to growth and build speed
- chance of sick (grows with age) or adventuring

TRADING:
- merchant only
- contribute to growth and build speed
- contribute gold
- chance of sick (grows with age)
(to consider: chance of mercantile expedition?)

ADVENTURING:
- away for some length of time (stop contributing)
- chance of TREASURE, SICK or DEAD

TREASURE:
- contribute flat gold and/or an item
- revert to HEALTHY

SICK:
- stop contributing
- chance of HEALTHY or DEAD

DEAD:
- remembered in history, any items acquired may be lost
